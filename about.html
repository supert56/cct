About Cambridge City Tours

Explore the city with an expert guide

Yvonne and Manuel have developed tours for everyone who wants to explore Cambridge, to find out about this world famous city and its University.

We will walk you through history to see the splendour of the architecture, hear about artists, learn about writers and literature, about scientists and people who have spent time in Cambridge.

About the green badge guides

Associate Members of the Institute of Tourist Guiding

All Cambridge City Tour guides are qualified to show you this historic university city and are ‘Associate Members of the Institute of Tourist Guides’. Our guides regularly update their knowledge and expertise through a programme of personal and professional development.

They will entertain you, inform you and tell you stories about Cambridge that will bring it to life. The guides will keep you safe in the narrow streets, pointing out potential hazards and especially ‘bicycles’ that come in all directions. All guides have third party liability insurance cover.
