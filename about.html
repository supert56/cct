About Cambridge City Tours

Explore the city with an expert guide

At Cambridge City Tours we have developed tours for everyone who wants to explore Cambridge, to find out about this world famous city and its University. If you are visiting with your family or friends, with a school, college or society, or are in Cambridge for a conference or business meeting, tours can be tailored for you. All our tours are private for up to 20 people. The exception is the Gothic Revival Tour that has a maximum of 12.

We will walk you through history to see the splendour of the architecture, hear about artists, learn about writers and literature, about scientists and people who have spent time in Cambridge.

About the green badge guides

Associate Members of the Institute of Tourist Guiding

All Cambridge City Tour guides are qualified to show you this historic university city and are ‘Associate Members of the Institute of Tourist Guiding’. Our guides regularly update their knowledge and expertise through a programme of personal and professional development.

They will entertain you, inform you and tell you stories about Cambridge that will bring the City to life. The guides will keep you safe in the narrow streets, pointing out hazards and especially ‘bicycles’ that come in all directions. All our guides have public liability insurance cover.

Other languages

Cambridge attracts residents from all over the world, so let us know if you would like to book a guide to give a tour in another language, including: Spanish, French, German, Italian, Russian, Chinese, Japanese

For tours in other languages please add £10 to the price of the tour.
