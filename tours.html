Our tours

Explore the city with an expert guide

If you are visiting Cambridge or want to find out more about the city where you live, Cambridge City Tours has something for you.

When a tour attracts more than 20 participants, additional qualified guides will be appointed, so that everyone gets the most from the tour.

THE ESSENCE OF CAMBRIDGE

Our introductory tour will tell you about the history and take you to visit locations in the centre of Cambridge.

TOWN AND GOWN

This tour will take you inside one of the colleges (subject to college availability) plus time to see and hear about people and places around the city centre.

Historic Cambridge

Your walk through the historic centre of Cambridge will take you to see the Corpus clock, the RAF bar at the Eagle pub, Newton’s apple tree at Trinity, but also take you inside Kings college to see its magnificent Chapel and memorial to the Chinese poet and alumnus of Kings College, Xu Zhimo. Admission cost is included in the price.

Meeting Location: Outside the front of St. John's college

£25 per person

2 Hour Duration

CAMBRIDGE WALKING TOUR FOR AMERICANS

This is the only walking tour in Cambridge to focus on the people and history that bind Cambridge with the USA. Our tour will include a visit to the magnificent King’s College Chapel, admission cost is included in the price. Maximum of 10 participants.

Meeting Location: Outside the front of Emmanuel College.

£25 per person

2 Hour Duration

GOTHIC REVIVAL AND DECORATIVE ARTS

Join Yvonne to discover the designers, the architects, the artists, stained glass makers and local craftsmen who revived Gothic design in the churches and colleges of Cambridge in the C19th. This tour includes a visit to Queens’ college, admission cost included in the price. Maximum of 10 participants.

This tour is only available between 1pm - 5pm.

Meeting Location: At the entrance to Little St Mary’s church in Little St Mary’s Lane.

£20 per person
